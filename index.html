Importance of Vaccination

Why Vaccinate?

Vaccination is a simple, safe, and effective way of protecting people against harmful diseases before they come into contact with them.

  • Prevents the spread of infectious diseases
  • Protects future generations
  • Reduces healthcare costs
  • Ensures community immunity (herd immunity)

Role of Hospitals in Child Vaccination

Why Hospitals Are Crucial for Child Vaccination

Hospitals provide essential services for vaccination. They offer proper medical care, monitor schedules, and ensure immunization awareness.

  • Safe and clean vaccination environments
  • Advice and monitoring of vaccine effects
  • Timely vaccinations for children
  • Support immunization outreach efforts
